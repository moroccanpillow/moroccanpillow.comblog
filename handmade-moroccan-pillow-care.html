FAQS

How should I clean my handmade Moroccan pillow?

It is recommended to spot clean your handmade Moroccan pillow using a mild detergent and a damp cloth. Avoid soaking the pillow to preserve its shape and design.

Can I machine wash my Moroccan pillows?

Machine washing is generally not recommended for handmade Moroccan pillows, as it can damage the fabric and affect the intricate designs. Always opt for gentle hand cleaning methods.

What is the best way to store Moroccan throw pillows?

To store Moroccan throw pillows, keep them in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Use breathable storage bags to prevent dust accumulation and maintain their shape.

How often should I clean my Moroccan pillows?

It is advisable to clean your Moroccan pillows every few months, or more frequently if they are used regularly. Regular maintenance helps to keep them looking fresh and vibrant.

Are there any specific maintenance tips for Moroccan Handira pillows?

For Moroccan Handira pillows, avoid excessive washing. Instead, shake them out occasionally to remove dust and fluff them to maintain their shape. Spot cleaning is best for stains.
